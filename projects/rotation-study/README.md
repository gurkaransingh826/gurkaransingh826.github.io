## Rotation Lab Validation Study

## Project Objective
- The goal of this study was to verify the theoretical moment of inertia (I) of a physical rotational
  apparatus made in an AP Physics C: Mechanics Lab by creating a high-fidelity model in Autodesk Inventor.

## Technical Specifications
- Geometry: Modeled using precise caliper measurements (outside radius = 11.150 mm, 
  inside radius = 8.755 mm).
- Material: Assigned PVC Plastic density (approx 1.38 g/cm^3) to the model.
- Mass Verification: Inventor physical properties engine verified the component mass at 
  0.069 lbs (~31.3g).
- Accuracy: This digital validation supported the experimental results margin in the lab of 2.7% error.
